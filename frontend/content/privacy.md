## Cookie Policy

We use a small number of cookies that are necessary for core application functionality and user preferences.

### Cookies We Use

#### `sidebar_state`

This cookie stores whether the application's sidebar is open or closed so the interface can remember your preference between visits.

#### `cookie_consent`

This cookie stores your cookie preference, such as whether you accepted or declined cookies, so we can respect your choice.

## What We Do Not Use Cookies For

We do not use cookies for:

- Advertising
- Analytics profiling
- Cross-site tracking

We do not place third-party tracking cookies on your device.
